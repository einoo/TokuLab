2018 GSA Bridsall Dreiss Distinguished Lecture

In 2018, the Birdsall-Driess Distinguished lecture was selected by GSA: The GEological Society of America. Associate Professor David Bouutt from Department of Geosciences, University of Massachusetts-Amherst would give a lecture on "The role of inter-basin groundwater flow and hydrogeological processes" at Kashiwa Campus, The University of Tokyo.
