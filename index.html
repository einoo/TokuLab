Welcome!

Our laboratory is aim to establish engineering capabilities for contributing future societies through the utilization of invisible subsurface environments and resources. Specifically, main targets are:

Securing energy, resources, and water;

Developing and utilizing the subsurface information;

Managing/protecting the subsurface environment.

Current research use the best approaches to handle wide variety of space/time dimensions with respects to subsurface environment. Multidisciplines are employed in our labroatory:

[1] Groundwater/solute migration and fate

[2] Long-term environmental change and stability of subsurface

[3] Importance of subsurface fluids on geodynamics

Our research fields include backend, energy security, management of surface/subsurface environment, and progress of earth system science.

Many activities were held in our laboratory. Every year we have field excursion to discover the interesting natural phenomenon.
