Main targets

We are aim to establish engineering capabilities for contributing future societies through the utilization of invisible subsurface environments and resources.

[1] Securing energy, resources, and water

[2] Development and utilization of subsurface environment

[3] Management/protection of subsurface environment: for sustainable development of our society; and harmonization with local society

Current research topics

Current research disciplines include:

[1] Groundwater/solute migration and fate

[2] Long-term environmental change and stability of subsurface

[3] Importance of subsurface fluids on geodynamics

Utilization of the best approaches to handle wide variety of space/time dimensions with respects to subsurface environment.

Activities

Our research fields include backend, energy security, management of surface/subsurface environment; and progress of earth system science.
